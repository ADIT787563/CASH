# Razorpay Verification Checklist

Razorpay manually reviews your website before activating your account. Ensure all these points are visible and working on your Vercel website.

## 1. Mandatory Pages (Must be visible in Footer/Menu)

- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: (`/privacy`)
- [ ] **Terms & Conditions**: (`/terms`)
- [ ] **Refund & Cancellation Policy**: (`/refund`) - **CRITICAL**
  - *We just created this. Make sure it loads.*
- [ ] **Contact Us**: (`/contact`)
  - Must show an **Email Address** (<wavegroww@gmail.com>).
  - Must show a **Phone Number**.
  - Must show a **Registered Business Address**.

## 2. Product/Pricing Information

- [ ] **Pricing Page**: (`/plans` or `/pricing`)
  - Must clearly show what the user is buying.
  - Prices must be in INR.
  - Plan details (Monthly/Yearly) must be clear.

## 3. Website Quality

- [ ] **No "Lorem Ipsum"**: Ensure there is no placeholder text on the homepage.
- [ ] **Working Links**: All buttons and footer links must work (no 404 errors).
- [ ] **Signup/Login**: The login flow should work (or at least show a "Coming Soon" if restricted).

## 4. Business Details (Match with Documents)

- [ ] The **Business Name** on the website must match your PAN card / GST certificate.
- [ ] The **Domain Name** should belong to you.

## How to Submit

1. Log in to Razorpay Dashboard.
2. Go to **"Account & Settings"**.
3. Under **"Website Details"**, enter your Vercel URL.
4. Submit for review. It usually takes 3-4 business days.
